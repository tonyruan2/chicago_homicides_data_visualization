Narrative Visualization of Homicides in Chicago from 2001 to 2020

Usage

Navigate linearly through the interactive slide show by clicking the 'Next' button.

Hover over each data point to see an informative tooltip.

In each slide, drill down further by clicking on a circle representing a year. A 'Year' modal containing information for the selected year will open.

In each 'Year' modal, click on a month to drill down even further. A 'Month' modal containing information for the selected (year, month) will open.
